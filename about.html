About Palms Pizzas

Late Night Energy. Real Pizza. King Street.

Built for Downtown Charleston Nights

Palms Pizzas was created with one goal in mind — to bring a true late-night pizza option to King Street in Charleston. Located at 471 King Street, this concept is all about simple food done right, served when people actually want it.

Whether it's after a long day, a night out downtown, or just a craving that hits late, Palms Pizzas is designed to be fast, consistent, and always satisfying. No overthinking, just good pizza in the right place at the right time.

Simple Concept. Strong Flavor.

The focus at Palms Pizzas is quality and consistency. Classic recipes, fresh ingredients, and a process built for speed without sacrificing flavor.

From traditional cheese and pepperoni to customizable options, everything is built around what people actually want when they’re in downtown Charleston — something quick, hot, and worth coming back for.
